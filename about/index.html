The unified visitor & access platform for Africa.

Most premises still run on a paper logbook at the gate — slow, easy to fake, impossible to audit. Sojaly replaces it with smart digital passes, calendar-based booking and verification that works with whatever tech you have — so any school, estate, church or office can move from logbook to digital, often in a single day.
